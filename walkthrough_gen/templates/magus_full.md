**Magus (6666 HP)**

* General notes  
  * Fire \> Lightning \> Shadow \> Ice  
  * M. Def Down resets every barrier change  
  * Change barrier after any attack (physical or magical) or 2 Masamune attacks  
  * After 2222 dmg will go to phase 2 (4 Fire Swords approximately)  
* Phase 1  
  * Crono Barrier Sphere self \+ Lucca Attack (barrier change)  
  * Keep Crono / Lucca \> 150 HP (Mid-Potion)  
  * Only revive Frog when good barrier is up  
  * If Fire / Lightning barrier  
    * Frog Attack \+ Fire Sword / Lightning  
  * Else  
    * Attack (barrier change)  
  * Once he doesn't change barrier he's locked into last one  
    * Fire Sword / Lightning until "begin casting a spell"  
* Phase 2  
  * Keep Crono full HP (Crono Mid-Potion \+ Lucca Fire if needed)  
  * Keep using Fire Sword  
  * Dark Matter used (safe strat)  
    * Crono Athenian Water Frog then Lucca  
    * Crono Mid-Potion self  
    * Lucca Mid-Potion Crono or Fire Magus (if Barrier was up)  
    * Keep using Fire Sword  
  * Dark Matter used (risky strat)  
    * If changed phases with Fire it's safer to do the risky strat (1 dark matter)  
    * Crono Athenian Water Frog then Lucca  
    * Keep using Fire Sword and pray
